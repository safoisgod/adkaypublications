Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our services

How long does the publishing process typically take?

The timeline varies depending on the service package and the current workload, but most books go through our process in 6-9 months. This includes editing, design, production, and pre-launch marketing. Complex projects may take longer, while straightforward books can sometimes be completed more quickly.

Do you work with first-time authors?

Absolutely! We specialize in helping debut authors navigate the publishing journey. In fact, about 40% of the authors we work with are publishing their first book. We provide additional guidance and support for first-time authors to ensure they feel confident at every step of the process.

What genres do you specialize in?

We work with a wide range of genres including literary fiction, mystery/thriller, science fiction, fantasy, romance, historical fiction, memoir, biography, business, self-help, and more. Our diverse team of editors and designers have expertise across multiple genres, allowing us to match each project with the right specialists.
