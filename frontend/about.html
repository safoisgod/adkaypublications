About A-D Kay

Accra, Ghana · Est. 2015

Est. 2015 · Accra

Publishing as Legacy

A-D Kay Publications was founded on the lifelong passion and unfinished vision of Kojo Ampem Darko, a teacher, writer, and self-publisher who dedicated himself to supporting fellow African authors since 2015. His formal plan to launch the company was interrupted by his passing in 2024.

His son, Nana Kwesi Safo, has taken up the mantle to realise this legacy. We are a fully digital, mission-driven publishing company that empowers African authors with end-to-end professional services while projecting authentic African stories to the global stage.

Every book we publish honours African ideologies, preserves cultural values, and drives literacy, inspiration, and positive societal impact.

Mission & Vision

What We Believe

Mission: To provide high-quality, accessible, and authentic publishing services that empower African authors to tell meaningful stories, preserve cultural values, and create books that educate, inspire, and positively impact society.

Vision: To become a leading African publishing company that projects African stories to the global stage, nurtures emerging writers into mainstream voices, and rekindles a culture of reading for knowledge, purpose, and social change.

01

Quality & Authenticity: Excellence at every stage while championing genuine African voices and narratives.

02

Accessibility & Impact: Making professional publishing attainable for emerging voices and prioritising content that educates and fosters positive growth.

03

Legacy & Integrity: Honouring our founder’s purpose through honest, transparent partnerships and cultural preservation.

Legacy in Motion

Our Journey

2014

Legacy Begins

Kojo Ampem Darko begins his journey by informally supporting African authors with manuscript development, guidance, and self-publishing assistance.

2015

First Book Launch

The first official title, Killed by Illiteracy, is successfully published and launched, marking a major milestone in the publishing journey.

2020

Introduction of Author Support Group

A structured author support group is introduced to mentor, guide, and empower emerging writers through collaboration, training, and shared resources.

2023

Commemoration of 8th Book Launch and 10,000 Books Sold

A major milestone is celebrated with the launch of the 8th book and surpassing 10,000 copies sold, reflecting growing impact and readership.

2024

Passing of Founder Kojo-Ampem Darko

The organization honors the legacy and contributions of its founder, whose vision and dedication laid the foundation for its continued growth and impact.

2026

Relaunch of A-D Kay Publications and A-D Kay Graphics Team

The brand is revitalized with a renewed vision, expanding its publishing and creative services while continuing to support African authors and storytellers.
