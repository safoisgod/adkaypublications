Who We Are

Crafting literary excellence since 2008

PageCraft Publishing began with a simple yet powerful vision: to create a sanctuary for exceptional storytelling in an increasingly noisy world. Founded in 2008 by literary enthusiasts Elena Rodriguez and Thomas Bennett, we started as a small collective of writers, editors, and designers passionate about bringing meaningful stories to life.

Today, we've grown into an award-winning independent publishing house, but our core mission remains unchanged. We believe books are not just products, but cultural artifacts that shape minds and preserve human experience. Each title we publish is treated with the care and attention it deserves, from initial manuscript to finished book.

What sets us apart is our collaborative approach. We work closely with authors through every stage of the publishing process, ensuring their vision is realized while bringing our expertise in crafting beautiful, market-ready books.

Our Journey

Milestones that shaped our story

2008

Foundation

PageCraft Publishing founded in a small Brooklyn loft with just three staff members and a vision to transform publishing.

2011

First Bestseller

"Whispers in the Dark" becomes our first New York Times bestseller, establishing our reputation for quality fiction.

2015

Expansion

We expanded our team and moved to our current headquarters, adding dedicated design and marketing departments.

2018

Literary Recognition

"The Silent River" wins the National Book Award, marking our commitment to literary excellence.

2022

Digital Transformation

Launched our enhanced digital publishing platform, making our catalog accessible worldwide.

2023

Present Day

Over 280 books published, 36 literary awards won, and a growing community of authors and readers.

Our Mission & Values

The principles that guide our work

At PageCraft Publishing, our mission is to discover, nurture, and amplify exceptional voices that enrich our cultural landscape. We believe in the transformative power of stories to connect people, challenge perspectives, and preserve the human experience.

Literary Excellence

We champion quality writing and maintain rigorous editorial standards for every manuscript we publish.

Author Partnership

We build long-term relationships with authors, supporting their creative journey from manuscript to finished book.

Beautiful Craftsmanship

Every book is a physical artifact of beauty, designed with care and produced to the highest standards.

Sustainable Practices

We're committed to environmentally responsible publishing, from paper sourcing to distribution.

Cultural Diversity

We actively seek and amplify diverse voices from around the world, representing varied perspectives.

Innovation

We embrace new technologies and approaches while preserving the timeless value of the written word.
