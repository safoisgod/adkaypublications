Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our publishing process

How do I submit my manuscript for consideration?

We accept manuscript submissions via email. Please send your complete manuscript along with a cover letter and author bio to submissions@inkwellpublishing.com. Our editorial team typically responds within 4-6 weeks.

What genres do you publish?

We publish a wide range of genres including literary fiction, mystery, thriller, science fiction, fantasy, historical fiction, and select nonfiction categories. Please visit our submissions page for detailed guidelines on the genres we're currently accepting.

Do you accept unsolicited manuscripts?

Yes, we welcome unsolicited manuscripts. However, please ensure your submission follows our guidelines and is sent to the correct email address. Manuscripts that don't follow our submission guidelines may not receive a response.

How long does the publishing process take?

The publishing timeline varies depending on the project, but typically ranges from 12-18 months from manuscript acceptance to publication. This includes the editing, design, production, and marketing phases of the book.

Do you offer publishing services for self-published authors?

Yes, we offer professional editing, design, and marketing services for self-published authors. Please contact our production team at production@inkwellpublishing.com for more information about our service packages.
